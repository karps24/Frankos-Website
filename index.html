Welcome to Franko's Golf Center

Franko’s Golf Center offers a relaxed, community-focused place for golfers of all skill levels to practice and improve. The facility provides a full driving range where visitors can work on distance, accuracy, and consistency, along with areas suited for beginners, families, and more experienced players alike. We're known for our friendly staff and affordable prices, making it an accessible spot for casual practice. The center also features professional golf instruction from PGA-certified coaches, giving players the chance to refine their technique with expert guidance. We also offer a wide range of proshop items and do custom club work and fittings. Overall, we're designed to be a welcoming environment where anyone can enjoy golf, learn new skills, and develop confidence in their game.
